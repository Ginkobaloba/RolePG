Dungeon Royale

You awake to find yourself in a strange room. You're not sure where you are, when you are, or frankly what you are at this point.
You have a conscience but apparently no phyical form.
Suddenly, you hear someone yell..."Prepare to fight!"
The first thing you need need to do is decide on your fighter's form.
You can be an alien, a ninja or a cowboy.

You have chosen to play as an Alien

Playing as an alien, you have a Laserblaster as a weapon.
Since it's from out of this world, it is a powerful weapon. Depending on your aim.

You have chosen to play as a Ninja

Playing as a ninja, you have a Katana as a weapon.
Your katana can slice through nearly anything. It must, however, be weilded with some skill and accuracy.

You have chosen to play as a Cowboy

Playing as a cowboy, you have a Winchester as your weapon.
This is a lethal weapon in the right hands. But know that it's accuracy is highly dependent on your prowess.

To get back to your "normal" self, You are going to have to fight and win some gold to pay to return to your own time and space.
And apparently your own body.

Things you are going to need to know before you fight are:

There are three types of attacks you can do with your weapon.
Normal Attack -- Your attack is accurate.
Quick Attack -- Your attack is quick and more effective.
Heavy Attack -- Your attack is most effective and more deadly.

When you are ready to fight enter Normal Attack, Quick Attack or Heavy Attack.
The choice is yours.
